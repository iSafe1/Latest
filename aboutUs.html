    About Us

    We are a passionate team of students from POLITEKNIK UNGKU OMAR, and we're excited to introduce you to our Final Year Project. Our mission is simple yet vital: to empower the next generation with the knowledge and skills they need to stay safe in the digital world.

    Our project revolves around the idea that cybersecurity awareness should start early. As technology becomes an integral part of children's lives, understanding online safety becomes as important as riding a bike or tying shoelaces. That's why we've dedicated our project to creating an engaging, educational, and fun platform for kids aged 7 to 12.

    We invite you to embark on this adventure with us as we strive to create a safer online world for the youngest members of our society. Together, we can inspire a generation of responsible and tech-savvy digital citizens.
